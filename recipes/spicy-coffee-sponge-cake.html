Spicy Coffee Sponge Cake

Description

This is a coffee flavored Spicy sponge cake.

It is soaked in Coffee Syrup

Lastly frosted with a swiss meringue buttercream frosing/p>

Spicy Coffee Sponge cake

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour - 220 gms
  • Cinnamon Powder - 15 gms
  • Caster Sugar - 190 gms
  • Eggs (Separated) - 8 pcs
  • Coffee flavor Essence - 2.5 mls

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 1800C or 3500F
  2. Grease and line a round 8 inch tin or two round 6 inch tins with greaseproof paper
  3. Measure and sift the flour and cinnamon powder in a bowl twice and set aside
  4. Place the egg whites in a large bowl and beat them until soft peaks form
  5. Beat in the Sugar, one or two tablespoons at a time until all the sugar is over and dissolved
  6. Add the coffee flavor and beat for a minute
  7. Beat in the yolks one at a time, beating until well incorporated.

    This can take 8 to 10 minutes and your mixture may turn a pale yellow.

  8. Sift the flour into the egg mixture in two parts an fold gently but quickly.
  9. When well mixed, pour the batter into the prepared pan/pans. place into the oven on the middle rack.
  10. Bake for about 30-40 minutes.

    Insert a toothpick or a metal skewer in the middle and make sure it comes out clean

  11. Remove the cake and allow it to cool in the pan for about 5 minutes, the turn it onto a cooling rack,

    peel off the greaseproof paper and let it cool completely.

Coffee Syrup

Ingredients

  • Brewed Coffee - 1/2 cup (Make sure to use when the coffee has cooled to room temperature)

Swiss Meringue Buttercream Frosting

Ingredients

  • Caster Sugar - 100 gms
  • Egg whites -large - 2 pcs
  • Vanilla Essence - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Unsalted butter - 70 gms (Cubed and Softened)

Directions

  1. Ina large bowl, pour in your egg whites and beat wit an electric mixer for a minute,

    then add your caster sugar gradually and beat until stiff peaks form,

  2. Place the large bowl with the meringue on top of a pot with simmering water -

    make sure the bowl does not come into contact with water.

    1. Continue beating the egg whites until the sugar dissolves

    2. If possible, have a cooking thermometer to check the temperature of the eggs,

      they should get to 1600F(700C)

      If you don't have a thermometer, the meringue should be hot to the touchand sugar dissolved

      - you should feel no grains when you rub it between your fingers

      When you reach that temperature; please remove the bowl from the heat and back onto your work table.

  3. Continue beating the meringue with the mixer untill it has formed stiff peaks and has cooled to room temperature
  4. Beat in the vanilla essence; then start adding a cube of softened butter one at a time mixing very well after each addition.

    Dont worry if the mixture looks like its curdling or Separated; just keep on beating it and it will get back to a smooth consistency

  5. Use the frosting immediately. If it for later, place it in a container witha cover and refrigirate it.

Assembling the cake

  1. Torte your sponge cake into three equal sized layers using a bread knife
  2. Take a tray or a cake board and apply a small amount of frosting at the bottom to hold the cake in place
  3. Take the top layer of your cake and invert it onto the cake board
  4. Pour a few tablespoons of the coffee syrup to wet the inside of the cake - you can use a pastry brush for full coverage
  5. Apply some frosting on the torte using a spoon or a spatula.
  6. Take the second layer of cake and repeat the same process. Take the final layer and wet it with the leftover syrup.

    Then decorate the whole cake with a thick layer of frosting all around

  7. Take the piping bag with some frosting and pipe a border around your cake.

    To garnish, you can sprinkle some cinnamon powder on top of the cake.
