White Forest Cake

Description

This is a Vanilla sponge cake.

It is soaked in Strawberry Syrup and filled with chopped strawberries

Lastly frosted with whipped cream or fresh cream and covered in white chocolate shavings

Vanilla Sponge cake

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour - 240 gms
  • Caster Sugar - 195 gms
  • Eggs (Separated) - 8 pcs
  • Vanilla Essence - 5 mls

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 1800C or 3500F
  2. Grease and line a round 7 inch tin or two round 6 inch tins with greaseproof paper
  3. Measure and sift the flour in a bowl twice and set aside
  4. Place the egg whites in a large bowl and beat them until soft peaks form
  5. Beat in the caster Sugar, one or two tablespoons at a time until all the sugar is over and dissolved
  6. Add the Vanilla and beat for a minute
  7. Beat in the yolks one at a time, beating until well incorporated.

    This can take 8 to 10 minutes and your mixture may turn a pale yellow.

  8. To ensure your batter is ready to add flour, check to see if it has reached the 'Ribbon Stage'.

    When you lift your egg mixture with your whisk or beater, it should pour and leave a notable

    streaks/shapes that do not dissolve - this is the ribbon stage.

    If the mixture dissolves, keep beating until it gets to the ribbon stage.

    If it is a liquid and won't get back to the ribbon stage, you may have to restart

    since this means that you batter has totally deflated.

  9. Sift the flour into the egg mixture in two parts an fold gently but quickly.
  10. When well mixed, pour the batter into the prepared pan/pans. place into the oven on the middle rack.
  11. Bake for about 35-45 minutes.

    Insert a toothpick or a metal skewer in the middle and make sure it comes out clean

  12. Remove the cake and allow it to cool in the pan for about 5 minutes, the turn it onto a cooling rack,

    peel off the greaseproof paper and let it cool completely.

Whipped Cream/ Fresh Cream Frosting

Ingredients

  • Icing sugar - 1/4 cup
  • Non-Dairy cream frosting OR Double cream- 1 cup

    (Make sure to chill fro more than 4 hours before use)

  • Vanilla Essence - 1/2 teaspoon

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl, sift the icing sugar and set aside.

  2. Measure the cream into a large bowl and beat the cream until it forms soft peaks

    Do not overbeat the cream - it may become too stiff and difficut to apply the cream to your cake.

    If using fresh/double cream, do not overmix as it may separate or curdle

  3. Add your vanilla essence and beat for a few second
  4. Add the icing sugar gradually until it has a creamy consistency.

Strawberry Syrup Filling

Ingredients

  • Chopped/crushed strawberries - 100 gms
  • Sugar - 200 gms
  • Water - 300 gms

Directions

  1. Place a pot onto a stove an d pour the sugar and water to heat
  2. When to water starts to boil and the sugar had mostly dissolved, add the chopped/crushed strawberries.
  3. Stir slowly until the mixture starts boiling, then turn down and simmer for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Your mixture should be Syrupy and thick. Your Syrup is ready to use.

Chocolate shavings

Ingredients

  • White Chocolate bar or Compound white chocolate - 150 gms

    (Grate or shave using edge of a knife)

  • Strawberries: 6-7 for garnishing, 10 or more for filling

Assembling the cake

  1. Torte your sponge cake into three equal sized layers using a bread knife
  2. Take a tray or a cake board and apply a small amount of cream frosting at the bottom to hold the cake in place
  3. Take the top layer of your cake and invert it onto the cake board
  4. Pour a few tablespoons of the Strawberry syrup to wet the inside of the cake - you can use a pastry brush for full coverage
  5. Put ome cream frosting in a piping bag and make a border around the cake to create a dam.
  6. Apply some cream frosting on the inside of the dam using a spoon or a spatula;

    then take some chopped strawberries and place them on top

  7. Take the second layer of cake and repeat the same process. Take the final layer and wet it with the leftover syrup.

    Then decorate the whole cake witha layer of cream frosting all around

  8. You can now cover the sides and the top of your cake with chocolate shavings
  9. Take the piping bag with some cream frosting and make individual stars around the border of your cake.

    Take the whole strawberries and place them on top of the cream stars
